# Golden Route: The Albanian Alps
A stunning motorcycle tour through the heart of the Accursed Mountains, covering Theth and Valbona.

## Logistics and Water
There is plenty of fresh spring water available along the main trail in Theth, but you should carry a filter for higher altitudes. Fuel is only available in Shkoder or Bajram Curri, so make sure to fill your tank completely before entering the national park.

## Surface and Safety
The road from Shkoder to Theth is now mostly paved, but the stretch towards Kir is still rough gravel with some exposed sections. Watch out for rockfalls after heavy rain as the terrain becomes very unstable and dangerous for motorcycles.

## Highlights
The Blue Eye of Theth is an incredible natural pool that requires a short hike but is absolutely worth the effort for the view. Theth valley offers several high-quality guesthouses with traditional food and safe parking for your vehicles.
