# Product Vision

RouteMarket Atlas Engine is an internal production engine for creating high-quality route drafts.

The system should:

- discover route topics with real demand,
- score ideas by SEO, product fit, source availability, feasibility, and risk,
- collect and preserve sources,
- generate route research packs,
- produce structured route projects,
- prepare drafts for RouteMarket review and publishing.

The goal is not mass AI content. The goal is fewer, better routes with visible sources, clear uncertainty, practical guidance, and human review.

## MVP 1

MVP 1 covers Demand Radar and Research Pack:

- keyword expansion,
- mocked source collection,
- topic scoring,
- backlog persistence,
- route project folders,
- research brief generation,
- CLI,
- MCP skeleton.

## Later Versions

- route concept,
- POI extraction,
- GPX and GeoJSON generation,
- guide writing,
- media pack,
- RouteMarket publishing.
